The effect of surname length on workplace productivity

You may be suspicious regarding these counterintuitive results of how the length of someone's surname effects their productivity in the workplace. I was as well, so let us analyse this topic in more depth.

What exactly is the workplace productivity index?

The workplace productivity index is an averaged score of over 200 items that taken together give a quasi perfect measure of how much work an employee gets done in the workplace. I acquired a dataset that enables me to isolate factors which contribute to higher productivity. Let's have a closer look.
